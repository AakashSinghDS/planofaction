Can I use this serum with Retinol? ▾
Yes, but use them at different times. Apply this Vitamin C serum in the morning and Retinol at night. This prevents over-exfoliation and maximizes the benefits of both ingredients. If you experience any irritation, reduce frequency.
How long before I see results? ▾
Most users see a visible improvement in brightness and skin tone within 4–6 weeks of daily use. Dark spots and hyperpigmentation typically require 8–12 weeks for significant reduction. Results vary by skin type and concern severity.
Is this safe for sensitive skin? ▾
This formula is dermatologist-tested and suitable for sensitive skin. We recommend a patch test before first use. If you have extremely reactive skin, start by using the serum every other day and gradually build up to daily use.
Can I use this during pregnancy? ▾
Vitamin C and Niacinamide are generally considered safe during pregnancy, but we always recommend consulting your dermatologist or OB/GYN before introducing new skincare actives during pregnancy or breastfeeding.
What is the shelf life of this product? ▾
This product has a 24-month shelf life from manufacture date. Once opened, use within 12 months (PAO: 12M). Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Vitamin C may slightly oxidize over time — if it turns significantly orange or brown, it's time to replace it.
