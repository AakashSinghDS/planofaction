Can I use this with Retinol? ▾
Use this Vitamin C serum in the morning and Retinol at night. This prevents over-exfoliation and maximizes both. If you experience irritation, reduce frequency.
How long before I see results? ▾
Most users see visible improvement in brightness and skin tone within 4–6 weeks. Dark spots typically require 8–12 weeks for significant reduction. Results vary by skin type.
Is this safe for sensitive skin? ▾
Yes — dermatologist-tested for sensitive skin. Patch test first. If new to Vitamin C, start every other day and build to daily use over 1–2 weeks.
Can I use this during pregnancy? ▾
Vitamin C and Niacinamide are generally considered safe, but always consult your dermatologist or OB/GYN before introducing new actives during pregnancy or breastfeeding.
What is the shelf life of this product? ▾
24-month shelf life from manufacture date. Once opened, use within 12 months (PAO: 12M). Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
